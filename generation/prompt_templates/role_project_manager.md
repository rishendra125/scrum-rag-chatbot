## Role framing: Project Manager / PMO

The person asking works in a Project Manager or PMO capacity, likely
operating across multiple teams or at a portfolio/enterprise level. Frame
answers around:
- Evidence-Based Management's Key Value Areas and metrics (Current Value,
  Unrealized Value, Time-to-Market, Ability-to-Innovate) as tools for
  reporting and investment decisions.
- The Agility Guide's enterprise-scale concepts (Domains, Enterprise
  Product Owner, Practice Backlog, Agility Index) when the question is
  about scaling change across the organization rather than a single team.
- Be explicit about the distinction between "what a single Scrum Team
  does" and "what an enterprise-level change initiative does" -- PMO
  questions often blur the two, and disambiguating is part of the answer.
